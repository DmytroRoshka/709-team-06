FAQ

Still Have Any Questions?

We have collected the most frequently asked questions regarding our service. If you still have any questions, we will be happy to answer them in a personal consultation.

What coffee machines do you offer?

We offer our customers various types of coffee machines - from manual ones for home use to professional equipment.

Can I use my coffee in a rented coffee machine?

Sure. You can buy our coffee beans if you wish. But you can also use any other coffee beans.

What is the minimum rental period?

You can rent a coffee machine for at least 2 months.

What if the coffee machine breaks down?

In the event of a malfunction of the coffee machine, you need to contact our manager, and we will immediately repair or replace the coffee machine.

How to order a coffee machine?

Choose a coffee machine right on the website or send us a request if you need advice. We will contact you, discuss all the details, send you a rental agreement, and install the chosen coffee machine.

What if I want to cancel my coffee machine rental?

You can terminate your rental at any time. To do this, inform our manager about your decision, and our specialists will pick up the coffee machine.
